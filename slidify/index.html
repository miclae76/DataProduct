Description

The Shiny App I wrote allows you to compare stocks performance. I've limited the stock list to companies with Capitalisation > 10 Billions USD.

Figures are for the years 2012 to 2014.

Details

Based on real stock price history (Adj Close) renormalized on a 100 basis to allow compariso in return.
